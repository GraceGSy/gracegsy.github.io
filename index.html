Hello, I'm Grace. I am a third year PhD student in the College of Computing at Georgia Tech. My advisor is Professor Alex Endert.

I am interested in work at the intersection of AI, learning sciences and human-computer interaction. My current research focuses on developing visual analytics tools for explainable AI, and evaluating the effectiveness of these tools.
